Ramadan Daily Routine (Jessore, Bangladesh)

Weekday Routine (Monday – Saturday)
Time Activity
3:30 – 4:30 am Pre-Dawn:
  • Suhoor: Nutritious meal (oatmeal, eggs, whole-grain bread, yogurt, water)
  • Fajr Prayer & brief Quran/dhikr
  • Optional light stretching/meditation
10:00 am – 9:00 pm Work from Home:
  • Start work at 10:00 am
  • 11:00 am: 1-hour Exam Coaching session
  • Incorporate short (5–10 minute) study/video lesson breaks during work
6:30 – 7:00 pm Iftar & Maghrib:
  • Iftar: Break fast with dates, water, and a balanced meal (lean protein, veggies, whole grains)
  • Perform Maghrib prayer after Iftar
7:00 – 9:00 pm Work Resumes:

Finish your work day while integrating brief study breaks as needed
9:00 – 10:00 pm Main Study Session:

Dedicate one focused hour to video courses and practice. (Supplement with study snippets during work breaks to reach 6–8 hours daily)

10:00 – 12:00 am Isha & Taraweeh at Mosque:
  • Leave for the mosque by 10:00 pm (adjust travel time accordingly)
  • Perform Isha prayer and join Taraweeh (typically 10:30 pm – 12:00 am)
  • Return home after Taraweeh if energy permits a brief review or relaxation
Sunday Routine (Weekend Variation – No Work)
Time Activity
3:30 – 4:30 am Pre-Dawn:
  • Suhoor & Fajr Prayer with Quran/dhikr
  • Optional light exercise
Flexible Morning Extended Study:

Dedicate 2–3 hours in the morning for video courses and practice

Afternoon Afternoon Session:
  • Another 2–3 hours of focused study
  • Enjoy a light, balanced lunch (salad, lean protein, whole grains)
  • Extra spiritual/leisure time: reading, extra prayers/dhikr
6:30 – 7:00 pm Iftar & Maghrib:
  • Iftar with dates, water, and a balanced meal
  • Perform Maghrib prayer
After Iftar Evening Routine:
  • Attend mosque for Isha & Taraweeh (similar timing: depart ~10:00 pm to join prayers until midnight)
  • If energy allows, a brief review session or light workout after returning home
  • Relax and unwind with a hobby or leisure activity

Additional Recommendations

  • Food & Hydration: Focus on balanced meals rich in fiber, protein, and complex carbs. Drink plenty of water during non-fasting hours and limit caffeine.
  • Exercise & Weight Loss: Incorporate short bursts of exercise throughout the day and a dedicated workout session in the evening.
  • Spiritual Activities: Maintain daily prayers, extra Quran reading, and dhikr. Consider journaling your progress.
  • Breaks & Naps: Use short breaks and power naps (20–30 minutes) to help sustain energy levels.
