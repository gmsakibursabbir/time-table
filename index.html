Weekday Routine (Monday – Saturday)
Time Activity
4:30 – 6:00 am Pre-Dawn:
- Suhoor: Nutritious meal (oatmeal, whole-grain bread with eggs, yogurt, water)
- Fajr prayer & Quran reading/dhikr
- Light exercise (stretching/yoga) and brief study (optional)
- Option to return to sleep or quiet reflection
8:30 – 9:30 am Morning Preparation:
- Wake up (if rested from a nap) and prepare for the day
- Brief review of work tasks and plan mini study sessions
10:00 am – 9:00 pm Work from Home:
- Begin work
- 11:00 am: 1-hour Exam Coaching for the failed subject
- Take 5–10 minute breaks hourly; use a break (10–15 minutes) for a quick video lesson review (accumulate extra study hours during the day)
7:00 – 7:30 pm Iftar:
- Break the fast with dates and water
- Enjoy a balanced meal (lean protein, vegetables, whole grains)
7:30 – 7:45 pm Maghrib Prayer:
- Engage in spiritual reflection
7:45 – 9:00 pm Work Resumes:
- Return to work until the designated end time
9:00 – 11:00 pm Main Study Session:
- Dedicate 2 hours (or split into segments) for video courses and practice to reach 6–8 hours daily
11:00 – 12:00 am Evening Spiritual & Exercise:
- Isha prayer and, if possible, Taraweeh
- 20–30 minute home workout (bodyweight exercises, brisk walking, or yoga)
- Wind down for sleep
Sunday Routine (Weekend Variation – No Work)
Time Activity
Flexible Morning Morning Routine:
- Relaxed Suhoor (or healthy breakfast if not fasting)
- Fajr prayer and quiet reflection
- Extended study session: 2–3 hours of video courses and practice
- Light exercise: Walk, yoga, or other activity for 30–60 minutes
Afternoon Afternoon Session:
- Another study block: 2–3 hours of focused learning
- Enjoy a light, balanced lunch (salad, lean protein, whole grains)
- Spiritual or leisure time: Reading, extra prayers/dhikr, or personal reflection
Evening Evening Routine:
- Iftar with dates, water, and a balanced meal
- Maghrib and Isha prayers (Taraweeh if attending)
- Optional short review session (30 minutes) or light workout
- Relax and unwind with a hobby or leisure activity

Additional Recommendations

  • Food & Hydration: Focus on balanced meals rich in fiber, protein, and complex carbs. Drink plenty of water during non-fasting hours and limit caffeine.
  • Exercise & Weight Loss: Incorporate short bursts of exercise throughout the day along with a dedicated workout session in the evening.
  • Spiritual Activities: Maintain your daily prayers, extra Quran reading, and dhikr. Consider keeping a journal to track both spiritual and study progress.
  • Breaks & Naps: Short breaks and power naps (20–30 minutes) can help sustain energy levels.
